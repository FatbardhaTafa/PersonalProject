Traditional Albanian Cuisine

Tavë Kosi

Tavë Kosi is a delicious Albanian dish made with tender lamb or goat meat, baked in a creamy yogurt sauce flavored with garlic and fresh herbs. It's a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Fërgesë

Fërgesë is a traditional Albanian dish made with peppers, tomatoes, and cottage cheese, all cooked together to create a rich and flavorful stew. It's often served with crusty bread for a satisfying and hearty meal.

Byrek

Byrek is a popular Albanian pastry filled with layers of flaky dough and a variety of savory fillings, such as cheese, spinach, or meat. It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a snack, appetizer, or even a main course.
